ui.managers.PopupManager

PopupManager opens any component in a modal.

<button class="btn btn-success" id="btn4">Click here to show alert</button>

Javascript (on button click):

uses("panjs.ui.managers.PopupManager.html");
PopupManager.createPopup("app.Tpanel.html",{}, {closeOnMaskClick:true});

Open the console (F12): ui.manager.PopupManager and app.Tpanel.html are lazy loaded.
